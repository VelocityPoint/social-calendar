# LinkedIn Version

Most small service businesses are leaving money on the table — not because of bad service, but because of bad infrastructure.

A roofer who misses 3 calls a week loses roughly $90K a year. Not in revenue they rejected. In revenue that walked straight to a competitor.

This is the problem we built Velocity Point to solve. AI automation isn't just for enterprise. It's for the plumber, the HVAC tech, the electrician who's running a real business and needs real infrastructure — without a six-figure IT budget.

Three things that move the needle for service businesses in 2026:

1. **AI call answering** — every call picked up, qualified, and booked. No more voicemail.
2. **Automated follow-up** — leads that don't book immediately get nurtured automatically.
3. **Smart scheduling** — AI routes calls and appointments around your existing jobs.

None of this requires a technical team. It requires the right tools configured correctly.

That's what we do.
